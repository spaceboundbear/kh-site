Route for Stupid 100% KH 1.5 Beginner


Stupid 100% is a dead/not real category that only one person runs, me. Also, I've only ever done it twice. Then I fell off the face of the earth for 8 years. Now I'm back and want to do it again. This route takes a long time, and a lot of grinding/farming. The requirements are dumb and set by me and a couple of others. Basically completing Jiminy's Journal and then some. 

Stupid 100% Requirements
-------------------

All Character Entries
All Trinities 
All Dalmatians 
All Keyblades 
All Exclusive Staves And Shields (Excluding Rare Drop Goofy/Donald)
All Keyholes Sealed 
All Secret Bosses 
All 10 Postcards 
All Synthesis 
All Gummie Missions 
All Spells 
All Mini games 
All Cups 
Complete Ansems Journal 
Obtain All Summons

// Postcard Locations //

1.  Climb to the roof of the Accessory Shop.
2.  Hit the fan in the Item Shop.
3.  Blue Trinity near the Item Shop in District 1.
4.  Crack open the blue safe in District 1 after finishing the area the first time.
5.  Jump up to the awning of the shoe store in District 2.
6.  After locking the Traverse Town Keyhole, look at the paper on the wall in the Synthesis Workshop.
7.  Cast Thunder on the wires in District 3, then step on the three buttons in the Gizmo Shop to set it in motion. Examine the clock on the wall to get two cards.
8.  (see #7)
9.  Also after locking the Keyhole, climb to the top of the Gizmo Shop, follow the path from roof to roof, and take the secret entrance to District 3. There's a postcard in the corner there.
10. After clearing Monstro, Geppetto sets up shop in District 1. Go look in a pot on his shelf for the last card.
